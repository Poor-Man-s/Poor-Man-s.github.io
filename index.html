About Poor Man's Podcast

Poor Man's Podcast is a comedy podcast created by three young broke bois based in New Zealand. Each week we take a podcast genre, or classic topic, and try to put our own spin on it, keeping it (partially) fact-adjacent, and doing our best not to butcher it completely. Poor Man's Podcast: Your favourite podcasts done poorly. Not badly. Poorly. There's a difference.
